The Story

Not chasing the title. Setting the tone.

🍤 GP was the entry that emerged from the 2024 split with no fixed playbook and a refreshingly different priority list. Where Practice 🍤 and Scuderia Practice 🍤 went chasing titles, 🍤 GP set out to bring a different kind of energy to the grid — driving a Lamborghini Huracán GT3 with more flair than fear of the championship table.

The team's founding line-up, Matt Lawn and Russell Swain-Fossey, set the template: infectious enthusiasm, genuine love for the sport, and a refusal to let the result define the weekend. Podiums were never the point — showing the Huracán's pace with finesse, and having a good time doing it, was.

"Unbothered. Hydrated. Moisturised." Self-care and positivity, built into the brand from day one.

That philosophy is the reason 🍤 GP, rather than either of the championship-winning liveries, was chosen to carry the family name forward. As the flagship identity, 🍤 GP now represents the team in real-world karting as well as sim racing, with a roster that rotates rather than staying fixed to any one pairing — the constant is the name, the car, and the motto, not any single line-up.

If you're chasing podiums above all else, the championship-focused liveries are elsewhere in the family. If you're chasing close racing, good company, and a moisturiser in the cupholder, 🍤 GP is the one to watch.
